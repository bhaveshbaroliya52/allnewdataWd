    About This Items

  • Parle-G biscuits, a staple in many Indian households, are known for their affordability, simple ingredients, and widespread availability.
  • They are a popular choice for various age groups and are often considered a wholesome snack option. The biscuit was initially called Parle Gluco and later renamed to Parle-G in 1980, with "G" standing for "Glucose".
  • Taste: The biscuits have a generic, slightly sweet taste that is generally well-received by a wide audience, including children and adults.
  • Ingredient: Parle-G is primarily made with wheat flour, sugar, and vegetable oil, with other ingredients like invert syrup, leavening agents, salt, and emulsifiers.
